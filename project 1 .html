Brioche (in French: Brioche) is a luxurious French bread made mainly of flour, eggs and butter, in addition to milk, yeast, salt and sometimes sugar. Brioche contains a large amount of butter (400g per kg of flour). Brioche is often baked with fruit or chocolate chips. It is served on its own, or as a base for dessert, with many local variations in added ingredients, fillings, or toppings.
